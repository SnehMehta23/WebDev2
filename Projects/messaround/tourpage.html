Cities: Delhi, Agra, Ranthambore, Jaipur


Experience the best of India's famous Golden Triangle Tour which compromises Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur. Amber Fort is also known as Amer Fort which is one of the most visited tourist attractions of Rajasthan as well as one of the renowned Historical Place of India. You will be amazed by the awesome route and architecture of Amer Fort. Made from sandstone and marble, this amazing fort comprises a series of four courtyards, halls, palaces, and gardens. At its entrance lies the primary courtyard, known as Jaleb Chowk, where the king's soldiers assembled and paraded themselves around. Suraj Pol (Sun Gate) and Chand Pol (Moon Gate) lead into this courtyard. To the right, its Shila Devi temple which remains open from 6 a.m. until 10:30 a.m., and again from 4 p.m. until 8 p.m. Sacrifices were part of the temple rituals, as the goddess is an incarnation of Kali. Legend has it that human heads were originally offered to the goddess before she was persuaded to accept goats.
